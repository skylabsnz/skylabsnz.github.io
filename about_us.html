Jordi Blasco

Jordi started to get interested in astronomy since he was a young kid. He has been a very active astronomy advocate and science communicator for the general public. He has been vice-president of Galileo, the astronomical society of the University of Barcelona from 1999 to 2005.
Jordi has a licentiate degree (BSc + MSc) in physics with specialisation in computational physics from University of Barcelona (Spain). He is co-founder of HPCNow!, a global consulting company focused in High Performance Computing, High Performance Data Analytics, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence.
